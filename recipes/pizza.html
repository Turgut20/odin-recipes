Pizza Recipe

Description

The recipe for a perfect pizza staright from Neapol. The only thing that you need is some ingredients and oven!Look further for our belissimo recipe

Ingerdients

  • For the dough:200g of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • a tea spoon of salt and a tea spoon of sugar
  • Main ingredients:Pepperoni
  • tomato sauce
  • green pepper
  • 200g of gouda cheese

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients for the dough into a bowl
  2. Mix it up until it turns into homogenous mass
  3. Leave the dough to rise
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface
  5. When it gets a circular shape start adding main ingredients
  6. First comes the sauce
  7. Then we are adding one half of our prepared cheese
  8. On top of that we are adding some pepperoni and pepper
  9. Right after that we are putting our pizza in the oven that is 200 degrees already
  10. After 30 minutes we are taking our pizza out to put the rest of our cheese
  11. Then we are putting our pizza back in the oven
  12. Finally, we are taking out our pizza after 5 minutes.
Voila!Our pizza is ready to be eaten:)
